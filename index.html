Serengeti

The Serengeti, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Tanzania, is an iconic landscape that epitomizes the untamed beauty of Africa. Its vast savannahs stretch as far as the eye can see, dotted with acacia trees and teeming with wildlife. This is the realm of the Great Migration, one of the most spectacular wildlife spectacles on Earth, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and other animals traverse the plains in search of greener pastures.

Africa

Kilimanjaro, the tallest peak in Africa, is a wonder of nature that captivates adventurers and nature lovers alike. Its snow-capped summit rises majestically above the East African plains, creating a breathtaking sight. The mountain is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to diverse ecosystems, from lush rainforests to alpine deserts, each teeming with unique flora and fauna. Climbing Kilimanjaro offers a thrilling challenge, rewarding climbers with stunning views and a sense of accomplishment as they stand atop its iconic peak, Uhuru Peak, at 5,895 meters (19,341 feet) above sea level.
